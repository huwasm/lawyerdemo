Design 2: Stacked Layout — Desktop & Tablet

On desktop, the stacked concept becomes a persistent side-by-side split. On tablet, the collapsible thumbnail from mobile gets more breathing room — 200px preview with expand toggle. The document panel stays always-visible on desktop, while tablet preserves the collapse/expand interaction from the mobile design.

Section 1: Desktop (1280 × 800)

Browser window frame. Side-by-side split: left panel is the always-visible document viewer, right panel is the intake form. No collapsing needed at this width.
